Amsterdam Dungeon

A unique and interactive experience, the Amsterdam Dungeon takes visitors on a thrilling journey through the darker side of Amsterdam's history, featuring live actors, special effects, and immersive storytelling

Jordaan District

Known for its charming narrow streets, historic buildings, and vibrant atmosphere, the Jordaan district offers an eclectic mix of art galleries, boutiques, cafes, and restaurants.

Albert Cuyp Market

Amsterdam's largest street market, the Albert Cuyp Market offers a vibrant mix of stalls selling everything from fresh produce and flowers to clothing, souvenirs, and street food.

Heineken Experience

The Heineken Experience is located in Heineken's oldest brewery. The 90-minute tour of the interactive Heineken Experience will tell you about the history, brewing process, and marketing of the Netherlands' most famous beer brand.

Keukenhof

The most popular attraction in March, April, and May is the Keukenhof. The Keukenhof is a beautiful park full of tulips and flowers. It's easy to plan a day trip from Amsterdam. You can opt for organised excursions or travel to the Keukenhof yourself using public transport.
